We have a variable called ‘string’ holding the text “How much wood would a woodchuck chuck?”

What does the pattern /wo.+d/ match if applied to the variable ‘string’ ?

1) “wood would a wood”  
2) “wood”  
3) “would a wood”  
4) “wood would a woodchuck chuck”
