Dhavide Aruliah is an Associate Professor in the <a href="http://www.science.uoit.ca" target="_blank">Faculty of Science</a> at the <a href="http://www.uoit.ca" target="_blank">University of Ontario Institute of Technology</a> (UOIT). He holds a PhD in Computer Science from the <a href="http://www.cs.ubc.ca" target="_blank">University of British Columbia</a> (UBC) although was originally trained in Applied Mathematics and Physics. His research interests are in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scientific_computing" target="_blank">Scientific Computing</a> and <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inverse_problems" target="_blank">Computational Inverse Problems</a>.
